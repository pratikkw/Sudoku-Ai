What is Sudoku Ai?

Sudoku Ai is an AI-powered application that scans and analyzes Sudoku puzzles from images, detects errors, and provides accurate solutions.

How to use?

Upload an image of your Sudoku puzzle, and Sudoku Ai will scan and analyze it. The application will generate an accurate solution for you.
